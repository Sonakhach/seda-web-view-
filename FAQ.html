Security, Survelance , Investigation?

How fast can you start with an investigation?

We can start an investigation within 15 min, even if the investigation is in another country. However, we prefer to carefully collect and prepare any surveillance operation or any kind of investigations. Like everything else – good planning equals good results. Some investigations need immediate attention – an example is a missing person, or sudden security issue. In this case just call please our international desk for 24/7 operations, 365 days a year.
