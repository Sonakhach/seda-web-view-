Advanced Implementations

We don’t only deploy the most cutting edge, state-of-the-art security and surveillance equipment. We also use our own unique software and hardware solutions.

Invisible surveillance!

ISRA provides advanced implementations of surveillance and security technologies, tailored to the unique needs of our clients. We frequently provide installations that are completely invisible to a third party. Unique installations of this kind are very difficult to spot, also for people with experience of security technologies, and thus provide substantial advantage for our clients.

How does it work?

Our knowledgeable security engineers install sound or video recording equipment in furniture or objects typical to the surveyed environment, such as microwave ovens in the kitchen, toys in the crèche, or power sockets or outlets in just about any room. Installations of this type blend perfectly with their environment and resist detection. The same type of implementations can be installed in cars or other vehicles. Successful advanced implementation requires that our technicians are well informed in advance about the layout of your particular location. Make contact and discuss your particular needs!

Security screening of office or home & securing locations

ISRA protects your home, office environment or temporary locations from bugging, eavesdropping and other forms of integrity breaches or espionage. While digital technologies involve new risks to your corporate and private integrity, all of the old threats of acoustic bugging and video surveillance remain. Our corporate clients frequently turn to us to investigate and eliminate the risk of electronic eavesdropping, typically prior to the relocation to new offices or in advance of events in conference facilities.

Personal integrity preparedness

Individuals have different reasons to wish to control the amount of personal information that they make publicly accessible. ISRA helps victims of crime, high-net-worth individuals, celebrities and individuals who for other reasons take their personal integrity seriously, to monitor, control and reduce public exposure in everyday life, business and travel. ISRA boasts the state-of-the-art equipment and expertise to assist clients serious about their IT-security preparedness. Beyond swift and effective response to network breaches and other incidents, we help our clients improve their IT-security

Who needs physical protection?

The needs and circumstances that compel individuals to request our private security services differ, but we commonly accept assignments from executives and high net-worth individuals particularly exposed to risk. Another client category consists of individuals affected by stalking or other crimes, where the legal authorities might not provide the necessary security support. In each case, ISRA is there to listen, advice and provide for your particular security needs.
